About us

We understand the challenges of hosting events. Synchronizing and meeting tight deadlines can take away all the joy from the event for the Host.

Nyotaah, the idea, was born to take the pain out of hosting events. Nyotaah is a unique concept that takes the daily grind away from the Host's shoulders to manage the micro and macro activities of the event.

Designed in the digital world, with a few clicks, Nyotaah offers everything needed to host a successful event. The event could be a two people getting together for a Pizza or a thousand people Wedding or a ten-thousand-person Corporate Event.
